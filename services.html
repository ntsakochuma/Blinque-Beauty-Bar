Our Services

Welcome

Blinquè Beauty Bar is a successful medium size business established on the 1st of February 2021 by Gontse Chuma. This beauty bar offers a wide range of services from eyelash extensions, eyebrow lamination, microblading to waxing. Founder and manager, Gontse Chuma, had started this business with the vision of providing quality services for students and working women who do not wish to spend thousands of rands on beauty services. The studio is located in the heart of Pretoria east, making it both convenient and safe for clients to get pampered. From its remarkable beauty and cosmetic services offered at affordable prices to its safe location, this beauty bar definitely stands out from its more expensive competitors.

Our Skills.



Lash Extentions

We offer semi-permanent lash extentions that range from 10 to 25 cm long. Clients can choose between 3 lash extension sets, the lassic set, the hybrid set and the full Volume set.
These sets can be refilled for R100 - R150 depending on the prefered set.



Brow Lamination

Brow lamination is a brow hair texturing treatment. Our trained specialists perm the eyebrows making them softer and easier to shape... essentially more flexible, shiny and smooth ;)



Waxing

Our beauty specialists are trained to professionally wax any unwanted hairs from our clients faces from unwanted eyebrow hairs to sideburns and much more. We use hard wax as it is generally more gentle on sensitive areas and makes the process slighlty less painful ;)



Microblading

Microblading is the cosmetic tattooing techniquendone to the eyebrows by a professional to create a more dark amd full appearance. This procedure takes roughly a hour and lasts anywhere from 18 to 30 months (Close to 3 years). Clients will be charged R1 600 for the first session and R800 for every session following.
