Beauty, Cosmetic & Personal care

Blinquè Beauty Bar is a successful medium size business established on the 1st of February 2021 by Gontse Chuma. This beauty bar offers a wide range of services from eyelash extensions, eyebrow lamination, microblading to waxing. Founder and manager, Gontse Chuma, had started this business with the vision of providing quality services for students and working women who do not wish to spend thousands of rands on beauty services. The studio is located in the heart of Pretoria east, making it both convenient and safe for clients to get pampered. From its remarkable beauty and cosmetic services offered at affordable prices to its safe location, this beauty bar definitely stands out from its more expensive competitors.
